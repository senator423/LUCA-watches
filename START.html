Our Story

Born from the
Heart of Geneva

In 1971, Marco Luca set out to build a watch that could survive anything — a boardroom, a mountain summit, an ocean dive — without ever compromising on elegance. He succeeded.

Today, Luca Watches remains family-owned, with every movement hand-assembled in our original Geneva atelier. We have never outsourced. We never will.

— Marco Luca, 1971
