Our Story

138 Years of
Unbroken Tradition

Founded in the workshops of Paris, Horlogerie began with a single obsession: to capture time itself within a case no wider than a coin. Our founder, Henri Marchand, believed that a watch was not merely a tool — it was the truest measure of a person's relationship with the world.

Today, 47 master watchmakers carry that philosophy forward, each apprenticed for no fewer than seven years before they are permitted to assemble a movement.

— Henri Marchand, 1887
